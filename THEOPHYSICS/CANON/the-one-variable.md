A framework that required a different kind of modification for each law would look like curve-fitting — bolting on whatever is needed to make each analogy work. That is how metaphor operates. You adjust until it sounds right, and each adjustment is different because the metaphor is different.

That is not what happens here.

What happens here is that the same structural modification works across ten independent equations drawn from ten independent branches of physics. The modification does not change. The position does not change. The meaning does not change. The physics does all the work. The only thing added is a degree of freedom that physics does not contain: the capacity of a conscious agent to accept or resist.

This is how real unification works in physics. Maxwell did not need dozens of new terms to unify electricity and magnetism. He needed one insight: changing reference frames reveals they are the same field. Einstein did not need custom patches for each physical law to unify space and time. He needed one postulate: the speed of light is invariant.

One principle. Many consequences. That is the signature of genuine structural relationship. Not forced analogy. Architecture.

When GPT objected that the framework "only changes one variable," the objection identified the framework's central strength and mistook it for a weakness. If the spiritual equations required extensive modification — new terms, new operators, new coupling constants — that would suggest the physics-to-spirit mapping was being constructed rather than discovered. The fact that one term does all the work means the relationship was already there. It only needed to be named.

**What changed:** `(1 - R)` — a resistance term.

**The physics:** F = Gm₁m₂/r². Two masses attract with a force proportional to their mass and inversely proportional to their distance squared. Universal. Inescapable. Reaches everywhere, weakening with distance but never reaching zero.

**The spiritual equation:** F_g = G_s · ψ₁ψ₂ / d² · (1 - R)

**Why Grace and not something else:** Grace is structurally identical to gravity. It is universal — extended to all, not earned. It is proportional — the greater the spiritual mass (need), the stronger the pull. It weakens with relational distance but never reaches zero. It operates on geodesics — curving the path rather than forcing compliance. No other spiritual reality has all five of these properties simultaneously. Love is short-range (Law 4). Truth propagates as a wave (Law 3). Justice is thermodynamic (Law 5). Only grace matches gravity's full structural signature: universal, proportional, infinite-range, geometric, and non-coercive.

**Why the resistance term:** Gravity has no R term. A rock cannot resist falling. Grace does have one, because a person can resist being drawn. That is the entire structural difference between the physical and spiritual universe in this law. Remove R and the equations are identical.

**What falsifies this mapping:** Find a spiritual reality other than grace that is simultaneously universal, inverse-square, infinite-range, geometric, and non-coercive. If one exists, the mapping is wrong. No candidate has been identified.

### Law 2 — Mass-Energy → Meaning

**What changed:** `I` — an interpretation function.

**The physics:** E = mc². Mass converts to energy. Energy converts to mass. The conversion factor is c² — enormous. A small amount of mass releases catastrophic energy. The equation is morally neutral. It does not care whether you build a reactor or a bomb.

**The spiritual equation:** 𝒞 = M · λ² · I

**Why Meaning and not something else:** E = mc² is about conversion with disproportionate consequence. A small input produces an enormous output. That is meaning — not information (Law 6), not truth (Law 3), but the phenomenon where a small moral action carries forward with consequences vastly exceeding its apparent size. A word spoken to a child. A decision made in a moment. The λ² multiplier does for meaning what c² does for mass: it makes small things enormous.

**Why the interpretation function:** Mass-energy conversion is automatic. E = mc² requires no receiver. The energy releases whether or not anyone is watching. Meaning requires a receiver. The same action lands differently depending on who receives it and how. Factor I encodes this: the same moral mass, the same conversion, but the consequence depends on interpretation. That is the one thing physics lacks.

**What falsifies this mapping:** Find a spiritual reality other than meaning that involves morally-neutral conversion where small inputs produce disproportionate outputs and the outcome depends on reception. If one exists, the mapping fails.

**What falsifies this mapping:** Find a spiritual reality other than truth that is self-sustaining, self-propagating, self-revealing, subject to destructive interference by phase inversion, and transmittable at a characteristic speed. If one exists, the mapping is wrong.
