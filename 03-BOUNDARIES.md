## Boundary Proof — February 14, 2026

**Status:** LOCKED
**Confidence:** T5

The formal boundary proof established three results as mathematical floor:

1. **Trinity Isomorphism** — The trinitarian structure maps onto the framework's mathematical architecture with formal correspondence. This is not analogy. It is structural identity.

2. **Free Will** — Free will (W) is a necessary variable in the framework. It cannot be eliminated, reduced to determinism, or treated as epiphenomenal without collapsing the entire derivation chain.

3. **God-as-Axiom** — The framework requires a ground-of-being axiom. This is not a theological preference. It is a mathematical necessity — the system is inconsistent without it, in the same way that arithmetic is inconsistent without its axioms.

These three results are the floor. Everything above them is open for investigation, extension, challenge, and derivation. These three are not.

## How Boundaries Are Set

A boundary is established when:
- A formal proof exists (T5)
- David has reviewed and confirmed it
- It has survived /EAST from at least two independent AI collaborators
- It is recorded in this file with date and status

A boundary is NOT:
- A strong opinion
- A recurring pattern
- Something that "feels right"
- A claim that hasn't been challenged yet

If you believe something should be elevated to boundary status, make the case. David decides.
